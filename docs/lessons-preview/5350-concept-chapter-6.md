## Xac Suat Luong Tu va Nguyen Ly Bat Dinh

Trong do luong chieu sai laser interferometer, ban dat do chinh xac den nanometer. Nhung co mot rao can vat ly ma khoa hoc hien dai khong the vuot qua: **nguyen ly bat dinh Heisenberg**. Khong phai vi cong nghe chua du tot — ma vi ban chat cua tu nhien. Hom nay ta tim hieu tai sao electron trong nguyen tu khong co vi tri xac dinh, va dieu nay anh huong gi den cam bien ban dang dung.

### Mat do xac suat vi tri

Trong co hoc luong tu, ta khong noi 'electron o vi tri $x$' ma noi 'xac suat tim thay electron trong khoang $x$ den $x + \Delta x$ la:'

$$P = p_1(x) \Delta x$$

trong do $p_1(x)$ la **mat do xac suat vi tri** (probability density). Day la ham lien tuc, va toan bo xac suat phai bang 1:

$$\int_{-\infty}^{\infty} p_1(x) \, dx = 1$$

Tuong tu cho van toc: $p_2(v) \Delta v$ la xac suat electron co van toc trong $[v, v+\Delta v]$.

### Nguyen ly bat dinh Heisenberg

Dieu ky la: **khong the do vi tri va van toc cung mot luc voi do chinh xac tuy y**:

$$\Delta x \cdot \Delta p \geq \frac{\hbar}{2}$$

trong do $\Delta p = m \Delta v$ la do bat dinh dong luong, $\hbar = h/2\pi = 1.055 \times 10^{-34}$ J.s.

**Hau qua cho nguyen tu hydrogen:** Ban kinh Bohr $a_0 \approx 0.53$ A. Neu electron o trong nguyen tu, $\Delta x \sim a_0 = 5.3 \times 10^{-11}$ m. Do bat dinh van toc toi thieu:

$$\Delta v \geq \frac{\hbar}{2 m \Delta x} = \frac{1.055 \times 10^{-34}}{2 \times 9.11 \times 10^{-31} \times 5.3 \times 10^{-11}} \approx 10^6 \text{ m/s}$$

Day la toc do cua electron trong nguyen tu — khong co quy dao xac dinh, chi co **xac suat hien dien**.

### Phep so sanh voi ky su co dien tu

Hay tuong tuong ban dang dung laser interferometer do vi tri nguyen tu (ly thuyet). Photon laser can photon nang luong cao (buoc song ngan) de dat do chinh xac cao — nhung photon nang luong cao se **pha khoi** electron, thay doi dong luong cua no. Do la su danh doi khong tranh khoi: do vi tri tot hon thi do dong luong kem di.

O thang ky thuat ban dang lam viec (micromet, nanometer), nguyen ly bat dinh khong anh huong vi $\Delta x \cdot \Delta p \geq \hbar/2$ voi $\hbar$ cuc nho o thang macro. Nhung o thang nanometer (transistor 5 nm trong chip dieu khien), nguyen ly nay quan trong!

### Electron trong nguyen tu: Orbital khong phai quy dao

Ve nguyen tu hydrogen (1 proton, 1 electron): do bat dinh vi tri electron bang ca nguyen tu. Khong the noi electron 'dang o quy dao nay hay quy dao kia'. Chi co the noi: co xac suat $p_1(x)$ tim thay electron tai vi tri $x$. Hinh dang cua $p_1(x)$ la **orbital** (van dao).

**Diem mau chot:**
- Mat do xac suat $p_1(x)$: xac suat tim thay hat trong $[x, x+dx]$ la $p_1(x)dx$
- Nguyen ly bat dinh: $\Delta x \cdot \Delta p \geq \hbar/2$ — gioi han co ban cua tu nhien
- Electron trong nguyen tu khong co quy dao xac dinh — chi co orbital (phan bo xac suat)
- O thang macro (micromet tro len), nguyen ly bat dinh khong anh huong thuc te; o thang nano (transistor), no quyet dinh hoat dong linh kien
