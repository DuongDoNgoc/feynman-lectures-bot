## Luat Hap Dan Va Cac Dinh Luat Kepler

Tai sao ten lua hanh trinh co the bay tu mai dat, vong qua nhieu nuoc, roi danh trung muc tieu cach xa hang nghin km voi do chinh xac vai met? Phan lon la nho chung ta hieu chinh xac luc hap dan cua Trai Dat. Cung luat vat ly mo ta mat trang quay quanh Trai Dat, Trai Dat quay quanh Mat Troi, va ten lua bay tu a sang b — **luat hap dan Newton**.

### Ba dinh luat Kepler (quan sat)

Truoc Newton, Kepler (1609-1619) da quan sat va mo ta chuyen dong hanh tinh bang 3 dinh luat:

1. **Dinh luat 1 (Quy dao elip):** Hanh tinh chuyen dong tren quy dao elip, Mat Troi o mot tieu diem
2. **Dinh luat 2 (Dinh luat dien tich):** Duong noi hanh tinh — Mat Troi quet dien tich bang nhau trong thoi gian bang nhau (hanh tinh gan Mat Troi thi chuyen dong nhanh hon)
3. **Dinh luat 3 (Dinh luat chu ky):** Binh phuong chu ky ti le voi lap phuong ban truc lon:

$$T^2 \propto (\text{Ban truc lon})^3$$

### Luat hap dan Newton

Newton (1687) giai thich CA BA dinh luat Kepler bang mot cong thuc:

$$F = G\frac{mm'}{r^2}$$

trong do:
- $G = 6.674 \times 10^{-11}$ N m^2/kg^2 (hang so hap dan)
- $m, m'$: khoi luong hai vat
- $r$: khoang cach giua chung

**Dac tinh quan trong:** Luc hap dan:
- Luon la luc hut (khong co luc day)
- Giam theo binh phuong khoang cach (inverse-square law)
- Tac dong qua moi khoang cach (tuc thoi trong co hoc Newton — khong dung trong tuong doi tinh)

### Phep so sanh voi ky su co dien tu

Luat $F \propto 1/r^2$ xuat hien nhieu trong cong nghe ban:
- **Cam bien luc tu**: luc tu giua nam cham va sat giam theo $1/r^n$ (tuong tu)
- **Cam bien dung luong** (capacitive sensor): dien truong giua hai ban cuc ty le $1/r$
- **Radar**: cong suat phan xa giam theo $1/r^4$ (hai chieu di — ve)

Nguyen tac chung: **luc hay truong do nguon diem toa ra theo moi phuong giam theo $1/r^2$** vi dien tich mat cau tang theo $r^2$ (4*pi*r^2), nen mat do cong suat phai giam $1/r^2$ de bao toan nang luong.

### Tu Kepler den ten lua

Quy dao elip khong chi la mo ta hanh tinh. Ten lua dan dao (ballistic missile) sau khi tat dong co cung bay tren mot phan quy dao elip voi tam Trai Dat o tieu diem. Tinh toan quy dao ten lua la giai phuong trinh vi phan cho luat $F = Gmm'/r^2$ — bai toan ma Newton da giai 300 nam truoc.

**Diem mau chot:**
- Ba dinh luat Kepler mo ta chuyen dong hanh tinh: elip, dien tich deu, $T^2 \propto a^3$
- Newton giai thich ca 3 tu mot cong thuc: $F = Gmm'/r^2$
- Luc $1/r^2$ la hau qua cua hinh hoc 3 chieu — bat cu luc nao toa ra theo moi phuong deu giam $1/r^2$
- Quy dao ten lua dan dao la phan quy dao elip — ban chat la bai toan Kepler/Newton
