## Quiz: Luat Hap Dan va Cac Dinh Luat Kepler

**Cau 1:** Luat hap dan Newton $F = Gmm'/r^2$ cho thay luc hap dan phu thuoc khoang cach theo:

A. Ti le thuan voi $r$
B. Ti le nghich voi $r$
C. Ti le nghich voi $r^2$
D. Ti le nghich voi $r^3$

**Dap an: C** — Luc hap dan giam theo binh phuong khoang cach (inverse-square law). Day la hau qua hinh hoc: thong luong truong toa ra qua mat cau $4\pi r^2$ bao toan.

---

**Cau 2:** Dinh luat 2 Kepler (hanh tinh quet dien tich deu theo thoi gian) la hau qua truc tiep cua:

A. Bao toan nang luong
B. Bao toan momen dong luong
C. Dinh luat 1 Newton
D. Luat hap dan nghich dao binh phuong

**Dap an: B** — Luc hap dan di theo huong $\mathbf{r}$ nen moment luc = 0, momen dong luong bao toan. Bao toan momen dong luong truc tiep dan den $dA/dt$ = const.

---

**Cau 3:** Mot ve tinh nhiet tao quay quanh Trai Dat o do cao $h = 400$ km (ban kinh quy dao $r \approx 6778$ km). Neu chung ta muon dua ve tinh len quy dao cao gap doi ($r' = 13556$ km), chu ky quay se thay doi the nao?

A. Tang $\sqrt{2}$ lan
B. Tang $2$ lan
C. Tang $2\sqrt{2}$ lan
D. Tang $4$ lan

**Dap an: C** — Tu dinh luat 3 Kepler: $T^2 \propto r^3$, nen $T \propto r^{3/2}$. Khi $r$ tang 2 lan: $T' = T \times 2^{3/2} = 2\sqrt{2} \cdot T \approx 2.83T$.

---

**Cau 4 (Tu luan):** He thong phat hien va danh chan ten lua dan dao (ICBM) can theo doi va du bao quy dao. Phan tich nhung thong tin gi (do tu radar) la du de tinh toan diem roi cua ICBM, va gia thiet vat ly nao can thiet.

**Goi y tra loi:** Radar do vi tri $(r, \theta, \phi)$ va van toc (Doppler) tai mot thoi diem. Sau boostphase (khi tat dong co), ICBM bay tren elip Newton. Tu 6 tham so trang thai (3 vi tri + 3 van toc), co the tinh hoan toan elip quy dao. Giao diem cua elip voi be mat Trai Dat la diem roi. Gia thiet: (1) Trai Dat hinh cau dong nhat (thuc te can biet $J_2, J_4$... cho do chinh xac); (2) Khong khi loang o do cao cao (< 1% anh huong); (3) Khong co luc day (den hau bang co the bay). He thong BMEWS/SBX radar can khoang 30-60 giay tu khi phat hien den khi co quy dao du chinh xac.

**Q1:** Luat hap dan Newton: luc hap dan phu thuoc khoang cach theo:
- A) A. Ti le thuan voi r
- B) B. Ti le nghich voi r

- D) D. Ti le nghich voi r^3

**Correct:** C

**Explanation:** Luc hap dan giam theo binh phuong khoang cach: F = Gmm/r^2.

**Q2:** Dinh luat 2 Kepler (quet dien tich deu) la hau qua cua:
- A) A. Bao toan nang luong
- B) B. Bao toan momen dong luong
- C) C. Dinh luat 1 Newton
- D) D. Luat nghich dao binh phuong

**Correct:** B

**Explanation:** Luc huong tam => momen luc = 0 => momen dong luong bao toan => dA/dt = const.

**Q3:** Ve tinh o quy dao ban kinh r. Tang r len 2 lan, chu ky T thay doi:
- A) A. Tang sqrt(2) lan
- B) B. Tang 2 lan
- C) C. Tang 2*sqrt(2) lan
- D) D. Tang 4 lan

**Correct:** C

**Explanation:** Dinh luat 3 Kepler: T^2 ~ r^3, nen T ~ r^(3/2). Khi r tang 2 lan: T tang 2^(3/2) = 2*sqrt(2) lan.
