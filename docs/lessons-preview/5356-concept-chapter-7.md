## Hap Dan Newton: Ung Dung — Thuy Trieu, Hinh Dang Trai Dat va Phat Hien Sao Hai Vuong

Nam 1846, cac nha khoa hoc Leverrier va Adams tinh toan vi tri cua mot hanh tinh ma chua ai nhin thay. Ho gui tin nhan den dai quan sat: 'Huong kinh vien vong den toa do nay, quy vi se thay mot hanh tinh moi.' Ngay hom sau, dai Galle (Berlin) tim thay sao Hai Vuong (Neptune) cach chinh xac 1 do. Day la mot trong nhung thanh tuu phi thuong nhat cua vat ly ly thuyet — **du bao bang toan hoc truoc khi quan sat**.

### Hap dan giai thich hinh dang Trai Dat

Trai Dat hinh cau vi moi vat thu hut moi vat khac — Trai Dat co 'tu thu hut lai' thanh hinh dang nang luong thap nhat. Nhung Trai Dat khong hoan toan hinh cau! Vi quay, luc ly tam lam phong to o xu dao. Ket qua: Trai Dat la **ellipsoid dep**, ban kinh xu dao lon hon ban kinh cuc:

$$R_{\text{xu dao}} - R_{\text{cuc}} \approx 21 \text{ km}$$

**Ung dung ky thuat:** He thong GPS can biet chinh xac hinh dang Trai Dat (geoid) de chuyen doi toa do ve mat dat sang toa do GPS. Mo hinh WGS-84 dung trong GPS thuong dung ellipsoid tham chieu — sai khac voi geoid thuc te den 100 m o mot so vung.

### Thuy trieu (Tides)

Thuy trieu khong phai do luc hap dan Mat Trang **keo nuoc len** — ma do **do chech lech** luc hap dan giua phia gan va phia xa Mat Trang. Phan chech lech nay (luc thuy trieu) tinh te hon luc hap dan chinh rat nhieu.

Luc thuy trieu: $F_{\text{thuy trieu}} \propto M_{\text{Mat Trang}} / r^3$ — giam NHANH hon luc hap dan chinh ($\propto 1/r^2$).

**Ung dung ky thuat:** Thuy trieu anh huong den tuyen duong tau bien va lich trinh ra vao cang cua tau chien. He thong du bao thuy trieu dung co hoc thien van — chinh la vat ly Newtonian.

### Phat hien sao Hai Vuong bang tinh toan

Sao Thien Vuong (Uranus) quan sat thay di chuyen sai voi du bao tu luat Newton. Hai nha toan hoc (Leverrier o Phap, Adams o Anh) doc lap tinh: **phai co mot hanh tinh chua biet** keo sao Thien Vuong ra khoi quy dao. Ho tinh toan vi tri chính xác cua hanh tinh an nay. Ket qua: phat hien sao Hai Vuong (Neptune) nam 1846 — chinh xac den $1^\circ$.

**Phep so sanh:** Day giong het viec ban phat hien mot cam bien bi hu trong he thong dieu khien bang cach quan sat hanh vi bat thuong cua he thong ma khong can mo may ra. Vat ly Newtonian du chinh xac de 'do' khoi luong va vi tri cua mot hanh tinh chua biet chi tu nhieu loan trong quy dao cua hanh tinh ben canh.

### So giot xu tru (precession cua quy dao Sao Thuy)

Mot bat thuong ma Newton KHONG giai thich duoc: quy dao sao Thuy bi so giot (precession) nhanh hon du bao Newton khoang 43 giay cung moi the ky. Day can den **tuong doi tinh tong quat Einstein** de giai thich — la mot trong nhung bung chung som nhat cua GR.

**Diem mau chot:**
- Hap dan giai thich hinh cau cua thien the va hinh ellipsoid dep cua Trai Dat quay
- Thuy trieu do chenh lech luc hap dan (luc thuy trieu $\propto 1/r^3$), anh huong den tau bien
- Sao Hai Vuong duoc du bao bang toan hoc truoc khi quan sat — thanh tuu phi thuong cua vat ly ly thuyet
- GPS can mo hinh geoid chinh xac de chuyen doi toa do — truc tiep ung dung vat ly Newtonian
