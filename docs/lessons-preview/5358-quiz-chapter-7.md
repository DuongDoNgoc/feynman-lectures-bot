## Quiz: Ung Dung Luat Hap Dan Newton

**Cau 1:** Luc thuy trieu (gian biet giua suc hut cua Mat Trang len cac phan khac nhau cua Trai Dat) giam theo khoang cach theo quy luat nao?

A. $1/r$
B. $1/r^2$
C. $1/r^3$
D. $1/r^4$

**Dap an: C** — Luc thuy trieu la chenh lech luc hap dan giua hai phia, xap xi $\propto 1/r^3$. Day la ly do Mat Trang (gan hon) tao thuy trieu manh hon Mat Troi (nang hon nhieu nhung xa hon).

---

**Cau 2:** Trai Dat khong hoan toan hinh cau — bi dep o hai cuc vi:

A. Mat Trang keo hai cuc vao
B. Luc ly tam do quay lam phong to o xu dao
C. Trai Dat nguoi lanh tu bac cuc xuong
D. Lop manti bien dang do ap suat

**Dap an: B** — Quay quanh truc tao luc ly tam $m\Omega^2 r\cos\phi$, lon nhat o xu dao ($\phi = 0$), lam vat chat phong ra ngoai. Trai Dat la ellipsoid dep voi $R_{xu dao} - R_{cuc} \approx 21$ km.

---

**Cau 3:** Sao Hai Vuong (Neptune) duoc phat hien bang phuong phap nao?

A. Quan sat truc tiep qua kinh vien vong
B. Tinh toan tu nhieu loan quy dao sao Thien Vuong, du bao truoc roi moi quan sat
C. Tu do anh sao bien (stellar occultation)
D. Bung sang bat thuong (nova)

**Dap an: B** — Leverrier va Adams tinh toan vi tri Neptune tu nhieu loan quy dao Uranus. Sau do dai Galle huong kinh vien vong va tim thay dung vi tri du bao — thanh tuu phi thuong cua vat ly ly thuyet.

---

**Cau 4 (Tu luan):** Trong he thong IMU cua ten lua chinh xac cao, tai sao can ban do trong truong hap dan (gravity map) cua khu vuc bay? Sai so trong truong hap dan bao nhieu thi anh huong den do chinh xac muc tieu?

**Goi y tra loi:** IMU accelerometer do luc rieng $f = a - g$. Neu gia tri $g$ dung trong may tinh sai voi $g$ thuc $\delta g = 10$ mGal $= 10^{-4}$ m/s^2, sai so vi tri sau thoi gian $t$ la $\delta x = \frac{1}{2}\delta g \cdot t^2$. Sau 30 s: $\delta x = 0.045$ m (chap nhan duoc). Sau 300 s (5 phut): $\delta x = 4.5$ m (co the anh huong ten lua chinh xac). Cac nhien lieu dan dao co thoi gian bay 20-30 phut — tich luy sai so lon. Giai phap: gravity map do truoc bang ve tinh trong truong hap dan (GRACE mission) + cap nhat the line GPS khi co tin hieu.

## Quiz Questions

**Q1:** Luc thuy trieu (chenh lech hap dan Mat Trang) giam theo khoang cach theo:
- A) A. 1/r
- B) B. 1/r^2
- C) C. 1/r^3
- D) D. 1/r^4

**Correct:** C

**Explanation:** Luc thuy trieu ~ 2GM*R_E/r^3, giam theo 1/r^3. Mat Trang gan hon nen manh hon Mat Troi.

**Q2:** Trai Dat bi dep o hai cuc vi:
- A) A. Mat Trang keo hai cuc vao
- B) B. Luc ly tam do quay lam phong to o xu dao

- D) D. Lop manti bien dang

**Correct:** B

**Explanation:** Luc ly tam m*Omega^2*r*cos(phi) lon nhat o xu dao, lam R_xudao > R_cuc khoang 21 km.

- C) C. Stellar occultation
- D) D. Bung sang nova

**Correct:** B

**Explanation:** Leverrier/Adams tinh toan tu nhieu loan Uranus, du bao vi tri Neptune. Galle xac nhan bang quan sat.
