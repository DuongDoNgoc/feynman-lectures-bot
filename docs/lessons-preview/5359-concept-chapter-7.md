## Luc Hap Dan va Luc Dien Tu: So Sanh Cuong Do

Khi thiet ke cam bien luc cho he thong do luong chinh xac, ban lam viec voi luc tu (mN), luc don hoi (Newton), nhung ban co bao gio de y rang luc hap dan giua hai electron nho hon luc dien tu giua chung mot ti so khong lo: $4.17 \times 10^{42}$? Day khong phai so nho — day la mot trong nhung cau hoi mo lon nhat cua vat ly ly thuyet hien dai.

### So sanh luc dien tu va hap dan giua 2 electron

Hai electron, khoang cach $r$:

Luc Coulomb (day nhau):

$$F_e = \frac{e^2}{4\pi\epsilon_0 r^2} = \frac{(1.6 \times 10^{-19})^2}{4\pi \times 8.85 \times 10^{-12} \times r^2}$$

Luc hap dan (hut nhau):

$$F_g = \frac{Gm_e^2}{r^2} = \frac{6.674 \times 10^{-11} \times (9.11 \times 10^{-31})^2}{r^2}$$

Ti so (khong phu thuoc $r$!):

$$\frac{F_e}{F_g} = \frac{e^2/(4\pi\epsilon_0)}{Gm_e^2} \approx 4.17 \times 10^{42}$$

Luc dien manh hon luc hap dan **42 bac luy thua 10**. Day la mot cau hoi mo: tai sao tu nhien co hai luc co ban voi cuong do chenh lech khong lo the nay?

### Tai sao hap dan chi phoi vu tru?

Neu luc dien manh hon 10^42 lan, tai sao luc hap dan la luc chi phoi cau truc vu tru (thien ha, sao, hanh tinh)? Vi:

- **Dien tich co dau** (duong va am) — vat chat thuong trung hoa ve dien. Hai vat co $N$ nguyen tu: moi nguyen tu co $Z$ proton va $Z$ electron, nen luc dien net giua hai vat la ZERO (trung hoa).
- **Khoi luong khong co dau am** — luc hap dan chi hut, khong bao gio triet tieu nhau.

Ket qua: o thang lon (hanh tinh, sao), vat chat trung hoa dien => hap dan thang the. O thang nho (nguyen tu, phan tu), hap dan qua yeu => dien tu thang the.

### Phep so sanh cho ky su

Trong cam bien dung luong (capacitive sensor) ban dung de do khoang cach micromet: luc giua hai ban cuc nho la luc dien tu. Neu thay the cam bien dung luong bang 'cam bien hap dan' (tuong tu), can tang kich thuoc len $\sqrt{4.17 \times 10^{42}} \approx 10^{21}$ lan de dat cung do nhay. Ro rang bat kha thi. Day la ly do tat ca cam bien luc vi mo deu dung dien tu, khong dung hap dan.

### Ban chat tru tuong cua dinh luat vat ly

Feynman nhan xet: ta mo ta luc nhu the nao, nhung khong giai thich **co che** cua no. Newton khong giai thich tai sao hap dan ton tai — chi mo ta no bang cong thuc. Dieu nay van dung cho ca vat ly hien dai.

**Diem mau chot:**
- $F_e/F_g \approx 4.17 \times 10^{42}$ — luc dien manh hon hap dan 42 bac luy thua 10 giua hai electron
- Hap dan chi phoi vu tru vi dien tich trung hoa (co dau + va -), con khoi luong luon cong
- Cam bien luc trong cong nghe luon dung nguyen tac dien tu (khong phai hap dan) vi cuong do luc qua yeu
- Tai sao hai luc chenh lech khong lo? — mot trong nhung cau hoi mo cua vat ly hien dai
