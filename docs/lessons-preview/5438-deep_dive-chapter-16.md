# ## Nang Luong Co Quan Tinh: $E = mc^2$ — Phan tich Chuyen sau

*Source: Chapter 16 - Chapter 16 (Section 6) | [📖 Nguồn](https://www.feynmanlectures.caltech.edu/I_16.html)*

## Nang Luong Co Quan Tinh: $E = mc^2$ — Phan tich Chuyen sau

### 1. Nen tang: Khoi luong phu thuoc van toc

De hieu tai sao nang luong co khoi luong, ta bat dau tu ket qua da duoc chung minh: trong thuyet tuong doi dac biet, khoi luong cua mot vat chuyen dong voi van toc $u$ la:

$$m = \frac{m_0}{\sqrt{1 - u^2/c^2}}$$

trong do $m_0$ la khoi luong nghi (rest mass). Khi $u \to c$, khoi luong $m \to \infty$, giai thich tai sao khong vat nao co the dat van toc anh sang.

### 2. Suy dan cong thuc dong nang tuong doi tinh

Cong viec thuc hien len vat bang luc $F$ di chuyen quang duong $ds$ la $dW = F \, ds$. Ap dung dinh luat Newton tong quat $F = dp/dt$ voi dong luong $p = mu$:

$$W = \int F \, ds = \int u \, dp$$

Tich phan nay cho ket qua:

$$\Delta T = \frac{m_0 c^2}{\sqrt{1 - u^2/c^2}} - m_0 c^2$$

**Y nghia vat ly:** So hang dau $\frac{m_0 c^2}{\sqrt{1-u^2/c^2}} = mc^2$ la **tong nang luong** cua vat; so hang thu hai $m_0 c^2$ la **nang luong nghi**. Hieu so la dong nang thuan tuy.

### 3. Va cham khong dan hoi: Khoi luong tong hop tang len

Xet hai hat co khoi luong nghi $m_0$ va cham nhau o van toc $\pm w$ (doi xung trong he quy chieu tam khoi). Sau va cham, chung dinh lai tao thanh vat $M$ dung yen.

- Bao toan dong luong: tong dong luong = 0 (do doi xung) nen vat $M$ dung yen.
- Bao toan nang luong: $2 \cdot \frac{m_0 c^2}{\sqrt{1-w^2/c^2}} = M c^2$

Dat $m_w = \frac{m_0}{\sqrt{1-w^2/c^2}}$ (khoi luong tuong doi tinh khi dang chuyen dong), ta co:

$$M = 2m_w > 2m_0$$

**Ket luan:** Vat hop nhat nang hon tong hai hat ban dau. Phan du $M - 2m_0 = 2(m_w - m_0)$ chinh la dong nang duoc hap thu, da tro thanh khoi luong. **Nang luong co quan tinh.**

### 4. Phan hach hat nhan: Ung dung tinh toan nang luong

Khi vat co khoi luong $M$ vo thanh hai manh bang nhau, moi manh co van toc $w$ va khoi luong tuong doi tinh $m_w$. Khi manh dung lai, khoi luong nghi la $m_0$. Nang luong giai phong:

$$E = (M - 2m_0)c^2$$

Voi uranium-235: khoi luong nghi $M(^{235}_{92}U) \approx 235.044$ u. Cac manh phan hach dien hinh (Ba-141 + Kr-92) co tong khoi luong nghi $\approx 234.856$ u. Chenh lech $\Delta m \approx 0.188$ u $\approx 3.12 \times 10^{-28}$ kg. Nang luong giai phong moi phan hach: $E \approx 175$ MeV.

### 5. Vi du thuc te: Cam bien khoi luong trong he thong kiem soat

**Bai toan:** Ban dung can luc (load cell) piezoelectric voi do phan giai 1 microgram de theo doi su thay doi khoi luong cua mot mau vat lieu bi nung nong. Neu ban cung cap 1000 J nhiet luong vao mau, khoi luong tang bao nhieu?

**Loi giai tung buoc:**

Buoc 1: Ap dung quan he nang luong-khoi luong:
$$\Delta m = \frac{\Delta E}{c^2} = \frac{1000 \, \text{J}}{(3 \times 10^8 \, \text{m/s})^2}$$

Buoc 2: Tinh toan:
$$\Delta m = \frac{1000}{9 \times 10^{16}} \approx 1.11 \times 10^{-14} \, \text{kg} = 11.1 \, \text{fg}$$

Buoc 3: So sanh voi do phan giai cam bien:
- Cam bien: do phan giai 1 microgram = $10^{-9}$ kg
- Thay doi khoi luong thuc te: $\approx 10^{-14}$ kg — nho hon do phan giai 100,000 lan

**Ket luan ky thuat:** Hieu ung $E=mc^2$ hoan toan khong the do duoc bang thiet bi thong thuong. Chi trong cac thi nghiem hat nhan (voi may phan tich pho khoi luong — mass spectrometer do chinh xac ppm) moi co the xac nhan quan he nay. Dieu nay giai thich tai sao vat ly co dien da thong tri hang the ky ma khong ai ngo den $E=mc^2$.

### 6. Lien ket bao toan dong luong — bao toan nang luong

Trong thuyet tuong doi, hai dinh luat nay khong doc lap. Neu bao toan dong luong dung trong he quy chieu $S$, thi de no cung dung trong he $S'$ dang chuyen dong, can thiet phai co them bao toan nang luong. Chung tao thanh **bon thanh phan cua mot four-vector** duy nhat trong khong-thoi gian.

**Y nghia cho ky su co dien tu:** Khi thiet ke he thong dieu khien cho robot hay vu khi dan duong, ban lam viec o toc do cuc thap so voi $c$, vi vay cac hieu chinh tuong doi tinh la khong can thiet. Tuy nhien, khi thiet ke he thong radar, lidar hay GPS — trong do tin hieu di chuyen voi toc do anh sang — thi thuyet tuong doi tro nen thiet yeu. Dong ho tren ve tinh GPS chay nhanh hon dong ho tren mat dat (hieu ung tuong doi tinh tong quat), va neu khong hieu chinh, sai so tich luy se dat 10 km/ngay — vo dung cho dan duong micrometer.

**Bai tap mau:**

Mot proton (khoi luong nghi $m_p = 1.673 \times 10^{-27}$ kg) duoc gia toc den van toc $u = 0.9c$.

a) Tinh khoi luong tuong doi tinh cua proton.
b) Tinh dong nang cua proton.
c) So sanh voi dong nang co dien $\frac{1}{2}m_0 u^2$.

**Giai:**

a) $m = \frac{m_0}{\sqrt{1-(0.9)^2}} = \frac{m_0}{0.436} \approx 2.294 \, m_0 = 3.84 \times 10^{-27}$ kg

b) $\Delta T = (m - m_0)c^2 = 1.294 \times 1.673 \times 10^{-27} \times 9\times10^{16} \approx 1.95 \times 10^{-10}$ J $\approx 1.22$ GeV

c) Co dien: $\frac{1}{2}m_0(0.9c)^2 \approx 6.1\times10^{-11}$ J $\approx 0.38$ GeV

So sanh: Dong nang tuong doi tinh lon hon co dien khoang 3.2 lan o $v=0.9c$ — su khac biet rat lon, khong the bo qua.
