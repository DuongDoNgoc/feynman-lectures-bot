## Bien Doi Lorentz: Khong Gian va Thoi Gian La Hai Mat Cua Mot Thuc Tai

Ban da bao gio cam mot vat hinh chu nhat va nhin nghieng chua? Chieu rong va chieu sau ma ban thay thay doi theo goc nhin — nhung ban than vat khong thay doi. Thuyet tuong doi noi voi chung ta dieu ky la hon nhieu: khong gian va thoi gian cung thay doi theo 'goc nhin' cua nguoi quan sat, nhung theo cach ma khong gian va thoi gian tron lan vao nhau.

### Bien doi Lorentz: Cong thuc cot loi

Xet mot he quy chieu dung yen $(x, y, z, t)$ va mot tau vu tru chuyen dong voi van toc $u$ theo truc $x$. Toa do va thoi gian do trong tau vu tru $(x', y', z', t')$ lien he voi he dung yen boi **bien doi Lorentz**:

$$x' = \frac{x - ut}{\sqrt{1 - u^2/c^2}}, \quad t' = \frac{t - ux/c^2}{\sqrt{1 - u^2/c^2}}$$

(trong khi $y' = y$ va $z' = z$ — vuong goc voi chuyen dong khong thay doi).

Dieu dang chu y: $x'$ khong chi phu thuoc vao $x$ ma con phu thuoc vao $t$. Va $t'$ khong chi phu thuoc vao $t$ ma con phu thuoc vao $x$. Khong gian va thoi gian da bi **tron lan** vao nhau!

### So sanh voi phep quay trong khong gian thong thuong

Khi ban quay he truc toa do 2D mot goc $\theta$, toa do moi la:

$$x' = x\cos\theta + y\sin\theta, \quad y' = y\cos\theta - x\sin\theta$$

$x'$ phu thuoc ca $x$ lan $y$ — day la su tron lan cua hai chieu khong gian. Bien doi Lorentz lam dieu tuong tu, nhung **tron khong gian voi thoi gian** thay vi tron hai chieu khong gian.

Dieu nay goi y rang khong gian va thoi gian khong phai la hai thu khac biet ma la **hai mat cua cung mot thuc tai 4 chieu** goi la khong-thoi gian (spacetime).

### An du cho ky su co dien tu: He toa do cam bien

Hay nghi den he thong do luong cua ban voi nhieu cam bien encoder tren cac truc khac nhau cua robot. Khi ban thay doi goc gan dau do — vi du nghieng 30 do — gia tri $x_{encoder}$ va $y_{encoder}$ deu thay doi, du dau do thuc te khong di chuyen. Ban phai ap dung ma tran quay de chuyen doi ve he toa do chuan.

Bien doi Lorentz cung tuong tu — nhung 'phep quay' xay ra trong khong-thoi gian 4 chieu. Khi tau vu tru chuyen dong nhanh, no 'quay' trong khong-thoi gian, va phep do $x$ lan $t$ deu bi tron lan. Bo nao cua chung ta duoc toi uu hoa de 'bu' phep quay khong gian 3D (chung ta tu nhien biet rang vat hinh chu nhat van la hinh chu nhat du nhin nghieng), nhung chua bao gio tien hoa de bu phep 'quay' trong khong-thoi gian vi khong ai tung di nhanh gan bang $c$.

### Tai sao cam giac thong thuong that bai?

Feynman noi rat thu vi: chung ta luon 'nhin' the gioi tu mot goc co dinh trong khong-thoi gian, vi toc do song cua con nguoi cuc nho so voi $c$. Do nhu the ban chi duoc phep nhin mot to giay tu chinh dien ma khong bao gio duoc nhin nghieng — ban se khong bao gio biet to giay co 'chieu sau' theo phuong vuong goc.

Neu chung ta co the chuyen dong voi van toc dang ke so voi $c$, chung ta se 'nhin thay' thoi gian cua nguoi kia — khong phai theo nghia thay qua khu hay tuong lai, ma la khong gian cua ho duoc tron vao thoi gian cua chung ta va nguoc lai.

### Dai luong bat bien: Khoang cach khong-thoi gian

Du phep quay 2D tron $x$ va $y$, nhung khoang cach $r^2 = x^2 + y^2$ khong doi. Tuong tu, du bien doi Lorentz tron khong gian va thoi gian, nhung khoang cach khong-thoi gian (spacetime interval) khong doi:

$$s^2 = c^2 t^2 - x^2 - y^2 - z^2 = \text{const}$$

Day la 'khoang cach thuc su' trong khong-thoi gian — bat bien voi moi nguoi quan sat trong bat ky he quy chieu quan tinh nao.

**Diem mau chot:**
- Bien doi Lorentz tron khong gian va thoi gian giong nhu phep quay tron hai truc khong gian — nhung trong 4 chieu spacetime.
- Khong gian va thoi gian la hai mat cua cung mot thuc tai vat ly. Nguoi quan sat khac nhau 'cat' khong-thoi gian theo huong khac nhau.
- Dai luong bat bien (invariant) la $s^2 = c^2t^2 - x^2 - y^2 - z^2$, tuong tu $r^2 = x^2 + y^2$ bat bien duoi phep quay thong thuong.
