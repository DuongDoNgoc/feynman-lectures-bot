## Quiz: Bien Doi Lorentz va Hinh Hoc Khong-Thoi Gian

### Cau 1

Trong bien doi Lorentz, toa do $x'$ trong he chuyen dong phu thuoc vao:

A. Chi toa do $x$ trong he dung yen
B. Ca toa do $x$ lan thoi gian $t$ trong he dung yen
C. Chi thoi gian $t$ trong he dung yen
D. Khong phu thuoc vao gi — $x' = x$ luon luon

**Dap an: B**
*Giai thich:* $x' = \gamma(x - ut)$ — ca vi tri $x$ lan thoi diem $t$ deu xuat hien. Day la diem mau chot: khong gian va thoi gian bi tron lan qua bien doi Lorentz, tuong tu nhu $x$ va $y$ bi tron lan qua phep quay trong khong gian thong thuong.

### Cau 2

Mot dong ho dung yen trong tau vu tru chuyen dong voi $v = 0.8c$. He so Lorentz $\gamma = 1/\sqrt{1-0.64} = 1/0.6 \approx 1.667$. Neu dong ho tau do 1 gio troi qua, nguoi dung yen tren Trai Dat do duoc bao nhieu?

A. 0.6 gio (it hon — dong ho tau chay nhanh hon)
B. 1 gio (bang nhau — thoi gian tuyet doi)
C. 1.667 gio (nhieu hon — dong ho tau chay cham hon)
D. Khong xac dinh duoc

**Dap an: C**
*Giai thich:* Time dilation: $\Delta t = \gamma \Delta t_0 = 1.667 \times 1$ gio $= 1.667$ gio. Dong ho chuyen dong chay cham hon — nguoi tren Trai Dat thay dong ho tau chay cham, nen ho do duoc nhieu thoi gian hon cho cung mot qua trinh trong tau.

### Cau 3

Dai luong nao sau day la bat bien (invariant) duoi bien doi Lorentz?

A. Toa do $x$ cua su kien
B. Thoi gian $t$ cua su kien
C. Khoang cach khong-thoi gian $s^2 = c^2t^2 - x^2 - y^2 - z^2$
D. Van toc cua vat $v$

**Dap an: C**
*Giai thich:* Spacetime interval $s^2 = c^2t^2 - x^2 - y^2 - z^2$ co cung gia tri trong moi he quy chieu quan tinh — day la 'khoang cach thuc su' trong khong-thoi gian Minkowski. Tuong tu $r^2 = x^2+y^2+z^2$ bat bien duoi phep quay trong khong gian 3D.

**Goi y tra loi mau:** (a) Ve tinh GPS di chuyen voi $v \approx 3.87$ km/s — nho hon $c$ nhieu nhung du de gay hieu ung do duoc: dong ho tren ve tinh chay cham hon dong ho mat dat do time dilation. ## Quiz Questions

**Q1:** Trong bien doi Lorentz $x' = \gamma(x - ut)$, toa do $x'$ phu thuoc vao:
- A) A. Chi toa do $x$ trong he dung yen
- B) B. Ca toa do $x$ lan thoi gian $t$ trong he dung yen
- C) C. Chi thoi gian $t$ trong he dung yen
- D) D. Khong phu thuoc vao gi — $x' = x$ luon luon

**Correct:** B

**Explanation:** Bieu thuc $x' = \gamma(x - ut)$ cho thay ca $x$ lan $t$ deu anh huong den $x'$. Day chinh la ban chat cua bien doi Lorentz: khong gian va thoi gian bi tron lan, tuong tu phep quay tron cac truc khong gian.

**Q2:** Tau vu tru chuyen dong voi $v = 0.8c$ ($\gamma \approx 1.667$). Dong ho tren tau do 1 gio. Nguoi dung yen tren Trai Dat do duoc bao nhieu?
- A) A. 0.6 gio
- B) B. 1 gio
- C) C. 1.667 gio
- D) D. Khong xac dinh duoc

**Correct:** C

**Explanation:** Time dilation: $\Delta t = \gamma \Delta t_0 = 1.667$ gio. Dong ho chuyen dong chay cham hon theo quan sat tu he dung yen.

**Q3:** Dai luong nao bat bien duoi bien doi Lorentz?
- A) A. Toa do $x$ cua su kien
- B) B. Thoi gian $t$ cua su kien
- C) C. Khoang cach khong-thoi gian $s^2 = c^2t^2 - x^2 - y^2 - z^2$
- D) D. Van toc cua vat

**Correct:** C

**Explanation:** Spacetime interval $s^2 = c^2t^2 - x^2 - y^2 - z^2$ khong doi trong moi he quy chieu quan tinh. Day la dai luong vat ly thuc su, tuong tu khoang cach Euclid bat bien duoi phep quay.
